# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Homestead to be a welcoming, friendly, and harassment-free project for
everyone — regardless of age, experience, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Being kind, respectful, and patient.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for learners, families, and the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Sharing any child's personal data in issues, PRs, or discussions.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Scope

This applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and
in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project
maintainer via a private channel (e.g. the email in the repository profile).
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of
Conduct, and may ban repeat offenders.
